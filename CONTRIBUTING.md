# Contributing to Awesome-Learning-from-Experience

Contributions are highly welcome! To maintain the high quality of this list, please read the following guidelines before submitting a Pull Request:

## Submission Guidelines

* **Quality First**: Submitted works or papers should be representative or innovative within the field.
* **Consistent Formatting**: Please follow the format used in this list.
* **Concise Descriptions**: Descriptions should be streamlined, explaining why this resource is noteworthy.
* **Accurate Categorization**: Place the resource under the most appropriate heading.

## How to Contribute?

1. Fork this repository.
2. Add your resource under the corresponding category (it is recommended to sort by date).
3. Submit a PR with a brief explanation of why you are adding this resource.

## Checklist

* [ ] Do the links open correctly?
* [ ] Are spelling and grammar correct?
* [ ] Does the resource already exist in the list?
